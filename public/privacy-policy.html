Privacy Policy for Orbia

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Orbia ("the App") is a mobile game developed by Fernando Lugo. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection.

Information We Collect

Orbia does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users. The App does not require account registration, login, or any personal data to function.

Data Storage

The App may store game progress (such as high scores) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

Orbia does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Orbia does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
